## üåç **Live Demo Flow**

### **1. Global Overview (1 minute)**
- **Show**: Global bloom map with real-time data
- **Say**: "BloomWatch integrates data from multiple NASA satellites - Landsat, MODIS, and VIIRS - to create this real-time global view of plant bloom activity."
- **Action**: 
  - Zoom into different regions
  - Click on bloom markers to show detailed data
  - Highlight the NASA data attribution

### **2. AI Prediction Demo (1.5 minutes)**
- **Show**: Click "AI Prediction" button
- **Say**: "Our machine learning model, trained on decades of NASA data, can predict bloom intensity 30 days in advance with 85% accuracy."
- **Action**:
  - Select a city (e.g., Tokyo for cherry blossoms)
  - Click "AI Prediction"
  - Show the prediction card with confidence score
  - Explain the Random Forest algorithm

### **3. Anomaly Detection (1 minute)**
- **Show**: Click "Detect Anomalies" button
- **Say**: "We use statistical analysis to identify unusual bloom patterns that could indicate ecosystem stress, invasive species, or climate change impacts."
- **Action**:
  - Show anomaly detection results
  - Highlight high/low anomaly types
  - Explain the Z-score methodology

### **4. Climate Correlation (1 minute)**
- **Show**: Click "Climate Analysis" button
- **Say**: "BloomWatch correlates bloom patterns with NASA climate data - temperature, precipitation, and solar radiation - to understand environmental drivers."
- **Action**:
  - Show correlation coefficients
  - Explain strong/weak correlations
  - Highlight climate change implications

### **5. Citizen Science (1 minute)**
- **Show**: Citizen science observations
- **Say**: "We integrate crowd-sourced observations to validate satellite data and engage the global community in conservation efforts."
- **Action**:
  - Show citizen observations
  - Highlight species identification
  - Demonstrate community engagement

### **6. 3D Visualization (1 minute)**
- **Show**: Click "3D Globe" button
- **Say**: "Our 3D visualization provides an immersive way to explore global bloom patterns with time-lapse capabilities."
- **Action**:
  - Show 3D globe data metrics
  - Explain time-lapse functionality
  - Highlight WebGL implementation
